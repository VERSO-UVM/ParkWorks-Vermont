Vermont Park Development Resources

Organizations and agencies that support park planning, design, funding, and development in Vermont.

State Agencies

Vermont Department of Forests, Parks and Recreation

Website: fpr.vermont.gov

Description: The state agency responsible for managing Vermont's state parks and forests. Offers grants, technical assistance, and guidance for municipal park projects through programs like the Vermont Recreational Trails Program and Land and Water Conservation Fund.

Services: Grant funding, technical assistance, planning support, trail development guidance

Vermont Agency of Natural Resources

Website: anr.vermont.gov

Description: Oversees environmental conservation and outdoor recreation planning. Provides resources for sustainable park development and conservation-focused recreational facilities.

Services: Environmental planning, conservation guidance, permits, water quality resources

Regional Planning & Development

Vermont Council on Rural Development

Website: vtrural.org

Description: Helps rural communities develop and implement strategies for economic, social, and environmental prosperity, including outdoor recreation infrastructure.

Services: Community planning, funding guidance, networking opportunities

Regional Planning Commissions

Website: vapda.org

Description: Vermont's 11 regional planning commissions provide planning assistance to municipalities, including support for park and recreation planning.

Services: Land use planning, grant writing assistance, GIS mapping, community engagement

Conservation & Land Trusts

Vermont Land Trust

Website: vlt.org

Description: Works to conserve Vermont's farms, forests, and natural areas. Can assist with land acquisition and conservation easements for park development.

Services: Land conservation, easements, acquisition support, stewardship guidance

The Trust for Public Land

Website: tpl.org/our-work/vermont

Description: National nonprofit with Vermont projects focused on creating parks and protecting land for public use. Provides expertise in park planning, design, and funding.

Services: Park design, community engagement, fundraising strategies, project management

Professional Associations

Vermont Recreation and Parks Association (VRPA)

Website: vrpa.org

Description: Professional organization for Vermont's parks and recreation professionals. Offers training, networking, and advocacy for park development.

Services: Professional development, networking, best practices, annual conference

Vermont League of Cities and Towns

Website: vlct.org

Description: Provides education, advocacy, and services to Vermont municipalities, including guidance on parks and recreation facilities.

Services: Municipal training, legal guidance, policy resources, insurance programs

Funding Organizations

Vermont Community Foundation

Website: vermontcf.org

Description: Offers grants to Vermont communities for various projects, including parks and outdoor recreation facilities.

Services: Grant funding, philanthropic advising, community impact initiatives

Vermont Housing and Conservation Board

Website: vhcb.org

Description: State agency that funds conservation and recreation projects, including land acquisition for parks and trails.

Services: Grant funding, conservation finance, technical assistance

Trail & Outdoor Recreation Organizations

Vermont Association of Snow Travelers (VAST)

Website: vtvast.org

Description: Maintains Vermont's extensive snowmobile trail system and can provide guidance on multi-use trail development.

Services: Trail planning, maintenance guidance, volunteer coordination

Vermont Mountain Bike Association (VMBA)

Website: vmba.org

Description: Promotes and supports mountain biking in Vermont, including trail development and maintenance.

Services: Trail design consultation, volunteer trail building, advocacy

Green Mountain Club

Website: greenmountainclub.org

Description: Maintains the Long Trail and promotes hiking in Vermont. Offers expertise in trail planning, construction, and maintenance.

Services: Trail planning, construction workshops, volunteer programs, stewardship education

Technical Assistance

University of Vermont Extension

Website: uvm.edu/extension

Description: Provides research-based education and technical assistance on community development, including parks and recreation planning.

Services: Technical assistance, research support, educational programs, community development

Additional Resources

For federal funding opportunities, visit the Land and Water Conservation Fund (LWCF) program administered through the National Park Service and Vermont's Department of Forests, Parks and Recreation.
